# Privacy at 100tools

A plain-English summary of what happens to your data when you use **[100tools.site](https://100tools.site)**.

## The short version

> **Client-side tools process your input entirely in your browser.** Your text, files, and images never reach our servers. The handful of tools that genuinely need a server are clearly labeled on their tool pages.

That's the whole policy.

## What we don't do

- We don't ask you to create an account.
- We don't upload your input.
- We don't analyse the content of what you paste, upload, or generate.
- We don't set tracking cookies that identify you.
- We don't sell or share any user data — there is none to sell.

## What we do

- We use minimal, **aggregate** page-view analytics (counts, paths, referrers) — never the content of your inputs.
- Tool pages carry small display ads from a third-party ad network. Ad targeting is based on the **page topic** (e.g. "JSON formatter page"), not on what you type. Home and category pages are ad-free.
- We log standard web-server access logs (IP, user agent) at the CDN level, retained for a short period for abuse detection. These are not linked to any user account or content.

## The exceptions

A small number of tools fundamentally need a server (for example, capturing a screenshot of someone else's website, or fetching a YouTube thumbnail). These are marked **"Runs on server"** on their tool page. Even then, we don't store the inputs you provide.

## Third parties

- **CDN/Hosting**: standard static-asset delivery.
- **Ad network**: serves contextual ads on tool pages.
- **Analytics**: aggregate page views only.

No third party receives the content of your tool input from us.
